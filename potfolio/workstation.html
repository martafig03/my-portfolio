For this project, our goal was to design a working station to help an African cuisine chef develop her small catering business. We wanted to incorporate the visual identity we developed for “Jóia de África” in our final design, therefore we used our illustrations around the outside of the working station. Additionally, we believe it was important to include a cutting board, have storage space where she can store equipment, and a refrigerated box with lid to ensure the quality of the food and ingredients isn’t corrupted.

The final design was made in a 1:2 scale proportion, using light materials like the propylene and cork to ensure that the working station can be easily carried without causing any injury to the client.
