Kailani Island is a 3D, third person, single player, adventure game for multiple platforms like MacOs and Windows, the player will be perceiving the world as Leah, the main character. Leah Evergreen (whose parents died when she was very little), was raised by her grandmother, and finds a powerful device by accident. Seeking guidance from her grandmother, she learns of its potential to reshape the world. Tasked with completing the device, Leah uncovers Akamu, a scientist causing havoc to find a cure for his terminal illness. His factory disrupts the island's natural balance, eading Leah to discover his role in her parents' death.

The character will be able to navigate Kailani's seasonal landscapes, employing walking and jump mechanics to conquer platformer challenges, all while utilizing a powerful device to complete tasks and strategically defeating robotic birds and the formidable antagonist, Akamu.
